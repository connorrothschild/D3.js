Kanye West

Kanye West is a perplexing figure. His persona and personality have been the subject of interviews, books, podcasts, and documentaries.

We know Kanye has changed. But what about his music?

Other songs are happier.



'Breathe In Breathe Out' is the 13th track on Kanye's debut studio album The College Dropout. As with most songs on the album, it's a more jovial track. In it, Kanye discusses what he perceives to be the lifestyle of a rapper: cars, money, and fame.
